Dr. Vivek Gautam - Poet & Educator

About

Dr. Vivek Gautam is an acclaimed Hindi poet, writer, and dedicated educator, celebrated for his socially resonant and thought-provoking poetry that blends keen observation with profound inspiration.

His primary vocation is education, and he is presently engaged in teaching within Delhi's Vinod Nagar community. His academic commitment deeply informs and enriches his literary perspective.

Actively contributing to Hindi literature since 1986, Dr. Gautam has been a steadfast pillar of the literary world. Through the organization "Udbhav," he has tirelessly championed the promotion of literature and arts, nurturing new voices and fostering cultural dialogue.

Poetry Collections

The Chessboard of Time

A profound exploration of temporality and human existence through the metaphorical lens of a chess game, where each move reflects life's philosophical dilemmas.

With Timeless Words

A collection that bridges ancient wisdom with contemporary concerns, demonstrating how poetic language can transcend temporal boundaries.

Gandhi's Fourth Monkey

A powerful socio-political commentary that reimagines Gandhian principles in modern contexts, addressing the ethical challenges of contemporary society.

Purchased Sleep

A critical examination of consumerist culture and its impact on human consciousness, exploring how modern comforts often come at the cost of spiritual emptiness.

Themes & Style

Social Consciousness

Poetry that reflects and responds to societal structures, inequalities, and collective human experience.

Critique of Consumerism

Examining the emptiness of material pursuits and the loss of authentic human connection in modern society.

Moral Values

Exploring ethical dimensions of contemporary life and the erosion of traditional virtues.

Human Emotions

Deep, nuanced exploration of love, loss, longing, and the complexities of human relationships.

Dr. Gautam's poetry offers a penetrating exploration of human sentiment and a compelling critique of contemporary societal constructs—most notably consumerist culture and eroding ethics. His verses artfully mirror the existential voids of modern life and the evolving nuances of human connections.

Programs & Initiatives

Udbhav Literary Organization

Founded and nurtured by Dr. Gautam, Udbhav has been a cornerstone for promoting literature and arts since its inception. The organization hosts literary gatherings, poetry readings, workshops, and cultural events that foster dialogue and nurture emerging literary voices.

"A platform where words find their wings and voices their resonance."

Bindu Se Sindhu

Amid the COVID-19 pandemic, Dr. Gautam pioneered this successful online literary series, bridging global literature enthusiasts via Facebook and YouTube. The program transformed challenges into opportunities, creating a vibrant digital literary community that continues to thrive.

"From a single drop to the ocean of literary expression."

Social Engagement

In recent years, Dr. Gautam has actively shaped literary discourse around anti-caste poetry and social justice, underscoring his unwavering dedication to transformative, socially engaged art.

His work in this domain includes:

  • Curating and participating in literary programs focused on anti-caste discourse
  • Writing poetry that challenges social hierarchies and advocates for equality
  • Mentoring young writers from marginalized communities
  • Organizing readings and discussions that bridge literary and social justice movements
  • Collaborating with activists and scholars to create interdisciplinary dialogues

This commitment reflects his belief in literature as a vehicle for social transformation and his dedication to creating a more equitable society through artistic expression.
